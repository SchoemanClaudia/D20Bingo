How to play?

×

D20 Bingo! is a game of chance, with a twist on the classic bingo. Combining bingo mechanics with the unpredictability of a 20 sided die, you will race against the clock in this solo adventure. Your goal is to complete a pattern on a 4x4 game card, with each number determined by the roll of a D20. This blend of chance and perhaps favor from the dice gods offers a unique and thrilling experience for solitary play. Test your luck and see how quickly you can achieve bingo.


Objective:

Complete a bingo pattern on the game card before the timer reaches 00:00. Each new game will provide a fresh bingo grid to keep it interesting.


Gameplay:

Click 'Roll' to begin, a 45-second timer will start on first dice roll. Your dice will activate and roll automatically during the game. A number between 1 and 20 will appear every 3 seconds on your dice face.


Special Rolls:

20 = Saving Throw: Adds 10 seconds to timer.
1 = Death Throw: Subtracts 10 seconds from timer.


Marking Your Card:

Click on the rolled number on your card to mark it. Aim to complete a row, column, or diagonal line of 4 numbers.


Got Bingo?

Click 'Bingo' to validate your pattern. If valid, you win! If not, keep playing. Win by completing a pattern before the timer runs out. Lose if the timer ends and reaches zero.


Tips:

Adjust sound settings in site header to activate adventure ambience. Watch the timer and mark numbers quickly. Use extra time from rolling a 20 wisely. Enjoy the game and see how fast you can get a D20 Bingo!
