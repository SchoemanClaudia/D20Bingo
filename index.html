What is D20 Bingo?

D20 Bingo is a game of chance with a twist on the classic bingo. Combining traditional bingo mechanics with the unpredictability of a 20-sided die, you will race against the clock in this solo adventure. Your goal is to complete a bingo pattern on a 4x4 game card, with each number determined by the roll of a D20. This blend of chance and perhaps favor from the dice gods offers a unique and thrilling experience for solitary play. Test your luck and see how quickly you can achieve bingo!

How to play?

Objective:

Complete a bingo pattern on your 4x4 game card before time runs out.

Start your Game:

Click 'Start Rolling' below to see your dice and 4x4 bingo card. Adjust sound settings within header if you would like backround music.

Gameplay:

Click 'Roll' to begin. A 45-second timer starts, and the dice rolls automatically every 3 seconds. A number between 1 and 20 will appear every 3 seconds. Special Rolls: 20 = Saving Throw: Adds 10 seconds to timer. 1 = Death Throw: Subtracts 10 seconds from timer.

Marking Your Card:

Click on the rolled number on your card to mark it. Aim to complete a row, column, or diagonal. Click "Bingo" to validate your pattern. If valid, you win! If not, keep playing.

Bingo?

Win by completing a pattern before the timer runs out. Lose if the timer reaches zero without a bingo. Tips: Watch the timer and mark numbers quickly. Use extra time from rolling a 20 wisely. Enjoy the game and see how fast you can get a bingo in D20 Bingo!
